## Tensions

| Claim | Position A | Position B |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Attachment vs. temperament | Attachment researchers: SSP measures relationship quality | Kagan: SSP measures temperamental reactivity |
| Style stability | Prototype model: stable factor with fluctuations | Revisionist: fully context-dependent, no enduring prototype |
| Categories vs. dimensions | Pop psychology: 4 discrete types | Taxometric research: continuous dimensions of anxiety and avoidance |
| Cultural universality | Bowlby: biologically innate, universal | Keller, cross-cultural studies: Western independence norms encoded as "secure" |
| Polyvagal theory validity | Porges: testable, clinically useful hierarchy | Neuhuber & Berthoud: phylogenetic claims don't hold; Grossman: scientific basis weak |
| Mother as primary determinant | Classic AT: maternal sensitivity drives attachment | Harris, Kagan: peers, genes, class, culture all equal or greater predictors |
