# S6: Clinical Evidence, Critiques & Future Directions

## Evidence: Strong mechanism (meta-analyses, Science), moderate pilots (Stanford, Edinburgh), emerging RCT (JAMA 2025)
## Limitations: Small samples, selection bias, impossible blinding, short follow-up, no head-to-head
## Critiques: Unfalsifiability risk, researcher-advocate overlap, commercial incentives, reductionism
## Implementation: No dietitians in MH, no insurance codes, adherence challenges, equity concerns
## What works: Mito deficits measurable, BHB mechanisms clear, exercise validates pathway, metabolic health improves
## Field: Nature Mental Health 2025, Stanford since 2015, Harvard BSI, 75+ trials, CME training
## Timeline: Now (early adopters) -> 2-5yr (larger RCTs) -> 5-10yr (possible mainstream)
