# S5: Interventions

## Keto: 70-80% fat, <50g carb, 3-5 day switching. Stanford 75% recovery, JAMA RCT positive.
## BHB: HDAC inhibitor (HDAC1/3/4), NLRP3 inhibitor, BDNF upregulation, epigenetic modifier. Science 2013.
## Fasting: 16:8 or 5:2. Metabolic switching, autophagy, better adherence than strict keto.
## Exercise: PGC-1a -> mito biogenesis, BDNF, NLRP3 down. 2025 meta: "mainstay" for depression.
## Sleep: Autophagy, glymphatic clearance, circadian alignment. Often overlooked.
## Supplements: CoQ10, Mg, B vitamins, alpha-lipoic acid, omega-3. Variable evidence.
## Safety: Keto flu (1 week), electrolytes critical, medical supervision for psych meds.
## Sources: 11, STRONG
