# S3: Psychiatric Applications

## Depression: PET hypometabolism, all stress models show bioenergetic impairment, JAMA Psychiatry RCT 2025 (n=88)
## Bipolar: Cycling mito function, lithium affects mitochondria, Stanford pilot 75% recovery
## Schizophrenia: Strongest metabolic evidence. Drug-naive first-episode already show insulin resistance. Abnormal mito morphology.
## Anxiety: Amygdala/ACC energy demands, NLRP3-anxiety link, GABA from ketones
## Insulin Resistance: Multiple CNS roles (growth factor, memory, BBB integrity). Shared across disorders.
