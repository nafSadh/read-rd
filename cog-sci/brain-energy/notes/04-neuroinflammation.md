# S4: Neuroinflammation & Gut-Brain Axis

## NLRP3: Convergent node, elevated in MDD, BHB directly inhibits it
## Cytokines: IL-1b, IL-6, TNF-a elevated in depression, correlate with severity/treatment resistance
## Microglia: Chronic activation -> synaptic pruning, reduced plasticity. Self-perpetuating cycle.
## ROS Bridge: Mito dysfunction -> ROS -> NLRP3 -> cytokines -> more mito damage
## Gut-Brain: 90% serotonin gut-produced. Dysbiosis -> LPS -> systemic inflammation -> neuroinflammation
## SCFAs: Butyrate (itself HDAC inhibitor like BHB), maintains gut barrier
