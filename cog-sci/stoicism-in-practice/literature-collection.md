## Tensions

| Claim | Position A | Position B |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Reappraisal vs. suppression | Stoicism teaches cognitive reappraisal (healthy) | Pop "stoicism" teaches emotional suppression (harmful) |
| Empirical status | SABS validation, Stoic Week data support benefits | No dedicated RCTs, most evidence borrowed from CBT/reappraisal literature |
| Philosophical depth | Academic Stoics (Pigliucci, Robertson) maintain intellectual rigor | Pop Stoics (Holiday, social media) strip philosophy to motivational quotes |
| Dichotomy of control | Empowering framework for agency and focus | Potentially victim-blaming tool that ignores systemic injustice |
| Military application | Stockdale: Stoicism saves lives in extremis | Critics: militarizing philosophy, reducing ethics to endurance |
| Commercialization trajectory | Democratizes ancient wisdom, reaches millions | McMindfulness parallel: strips ethical core, serves capitalism |
