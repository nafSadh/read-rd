**Research coverage:**

- S4 (Misuse & Toxic Stoicism): Broicism, manosphere appropriation, emotional bypassing, dichotomy of control misuse, grief suppression
- S5 (Pop-Psychology Pipeline): Holiday criticism, TikTok Stoicism (257K videos, 2.5B views), McMindfulness parallel, what gets lost
- S6 (Integration & Clinical): Robertson's clinical work, Stockdale, military resilience (CSF/MRT), anxiety/anger/grief applications

**Decisions made:**
- Placed in `cog-sci/stoicism-in-practice/` — this is a cognitive science + clinical psychology project
- Critical lens (S4, S5) given equal weight to positive evidence (S1, S3) — balanced assessment
- Gross & John suppression/reappraisal distinction used as key framework for understanding toxic vs. authentic Stoicism
- SABS validation (LeBon et al. 2025) treated as milestone in empirical Stoicism
- McMindfulness parallel used to illuminate the commercialization trajectory
