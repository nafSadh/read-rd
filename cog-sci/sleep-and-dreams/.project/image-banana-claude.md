## s1-why-we-sleep.dd.html — Why We Sleep
- **Placement:** Hero at top
- **File:** `img/s1-hero.png`
- **Prompt:** A descending staircase of sleep stages like a hypnogram with a figure slowly descending into deeper levels, violet accent on the deepest step, a faint clock face in the background

## s2-sleeping-brain.dd.html — The Sleeping Brain
- **Placement:** Hero at top
- **File:** `img/s2-hero.png`
- **Prompt:** A cross-section of a brain with flowing streams coursing through it, the glymphatic system as a gentle river washing through tissue, violet accent on the fluid channels, small debris particles being carried away

## Notes
- Sleep imagery works beautifully in sketch style: waves, descending staircases, flowing fluids
- The glymphatic system is a particularly striking visual (brain being washed during sleep)
