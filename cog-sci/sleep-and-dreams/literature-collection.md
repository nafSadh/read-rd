## Tensions

| Claim | Position A | Position B |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Glymphatic system significance | Nedergaard: brain-wide waste clearance pathway, explains why we need sleep | Critics: mechanism specifics still debated, human evidence limited (5 surgery patients) |
| Why We Sleep accuracy | Walker: compelling synthesis of sleep science for general audience | Guzey: significant factual errors, overblown claims, cherry-picked studies |
| Dream function | Threat simulation (Revonsuo): dreams are evolutionarily adaptive rehearsal | Activation-synthesis (Hobson): dreams are epiphenomenal noise the brain tries to make sense of |
| Lucid dreaming therapeutic value | RCTs show 85% PTSD resolution, 30+ year track record for nightmares | Small sample sizes, replication needed, selection bias in participants who can lucid dream |
| Blue light as sleep villain | Melatonin suppression well-documented, 60-80% students affected | Effect size may be overstated, blue-light-blocking glasses show mixed results in meta-analyses |
| Sleep debt as concept | Walker: cumulative, can never fully repay | Recovery sleep studies show rapid restoration of most cognitive functions |
