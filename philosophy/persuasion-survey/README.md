# The Architecture of Influence — Survey Literature Collection

> A five-part taxonomy of persuasion: **Rhetoric, Exploit, Defend, Automate, Govern** — tracing the science of influence from Aristotle to AI

## What This Is

A structured literature collection and deep-dive analysis surveying the full arc of persuasion science:

- Classical rhetoric (Aristotle, Plato, Cicero) through modern influence psychology (Cialdini, Kahneman)
- Dark psychology and cognitive bias exploitation as weaponized persuasion
- 2,300 years of defenses: Stoicism → CBT → psychological inoculation → prebunking
- The LLM persuasion discontinuity: machines that match or exceed human persuasiveness
- Governance responses: EU AI Act, TAKE IT DOWN Act, deepfake legislation
