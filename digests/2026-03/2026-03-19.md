**Category:** Research Paper | **Source:** arXiv

### AI Cancer-Prediction Tool Achieves Better-Than-Expert Accuracy on Multicenter Dataset
**Category:** Industry News | **Source:** ScienceDaily

A new machine learning model combining tumor genomic signatures with multi-modal clinical data achieves better-than-expert accuracy at predicting cancer spread, validated across a multicenter dataset spanning multiple cancer types and hospital systems. The result is significant because multicenter validation — accounting for variation in imaging protocols, sequencing platforms, and patient populations — is the hardest test for clinical ML models and the one most closely tied to eventual regulatory approval. ### Humanoid Robot Plays Tennis with Human Partner at GTC
**Category:** Industry News | **Source:** CNBC / TheAIInsider

One of GTC's most widely shared moments was a live demonstration of the Galbot robot (running on a Unitree G1 chassis) successfully rallying with a human tennis player on the conference floor, achieving 96% forehand success in simulation after only five hours of training data. The demonstration was notable not just as spectacle but as a concrete benchmark: five hours of training data to reach near-human consistency on a dexterous, timing-sensitive physical task. Isaac Sim's role in generating the synthetic training environment was central to the result.
