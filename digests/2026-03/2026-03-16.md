## Research Highlights

### HiMAC: Hierarchical Macro-Micro Learning for Long-Horizon LLM Agents
**Category:** Research Paper | **Source:** arXiv

HiMAC introduces a hierarchical framework that separates macro-level goal decomposition from micro-level action execution, enabling LLM agents to reliably handle complex, multi-step tasks that flat planners consistently fail on. The paper validates the approach on a suite of long-horizon benchmarks and reports more than 40% improvement over non-hierarchical baselines. The macro-micro separation mirrors how human cognition handles planning, and the results suggest it may become a foundational pattern for production agent architectures.

[Read more](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00977)

### NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Super: 120B Hybrid Mamba-Transformer MoE
**Category:** Research Paper | **Source:** arXiv / Hugging Face

NVIDIA published full technical details on Nemotron 3 Super, a 120-billion-parameter hybrid model that pairs Mamba state-space layers with a Transformer MoE architecture, activating only 12 billion parameters per forward pass. The model supports a 1-million-token context window and delivers 2.2× throughput compared to GPT-OSS-120B on equivalent hardware, while open weights are available on Hugging Face. These specs make it one of the most inference-efficient large open models released to date, with clear implications for local enterprise deployment.

[Read more](https://huggingface.co/nvidia/Nemotron-3-Super-120B)
