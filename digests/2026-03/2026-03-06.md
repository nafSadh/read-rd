## Research Highlights

### Spatial T2I: Text-to-Image Generation with Explicit Spatial Reasoning
**Category:** Research Paper | **Source:** arXiv

Spatial T2I introduces a text-to-image model that builds explicit spatial reasoning into the generation process, giving users precise control over object placement through natural language descriptions rather than bounding boxes or masks. The approach parses spatial relationships from text ("the red chair is to the left of the window") and enforces them throughout diffusion. Evaluations show substantial improvements over baseline diffusion models on spatial accuracy benchmarks, with no sacrifice in image quality.

[Read more](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.04120)

### CubeComposer: 3D Scene Composition from Individual Object Descriptions
**Category:** Research Paper | **Source:** arXiv

CubeComposer extends text-to-3D generation beyond single-object synthesis to full scene composition, assembling complex spatial arrangements from individual object descriptions. The model encodes each object independently and then reasons over their spatial relationships to produce a coherent 3D scene, addressing a longstanding limitation of 3D generative models. This is an important step toward generating environments — not just props — which matters for simulation, gaming, and embodied AI research.

[Read more](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.04317)
