# Literature Collection — Ice Cream Culture
## An Anthropological Survey: Technology, Social Space, Cultural Artifact, Industry

**Project:** How has ice cream — as technology, social space, cultural artifact, and industry — shaped and been shaped by the societies that consumed it?
