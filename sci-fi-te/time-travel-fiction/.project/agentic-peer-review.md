1. **No section hero images.** S1-S6 and the synthesis paper lack hero images. Prompts exist in image-banana-claude.md but have not been executed. The overview has a hero image. This creates visual inconsistency across the project.

2. **Limited non-Western coverage persists.** The round 1 recommendation to expand non-Western sources has not been addressed. S6 includes a "Global Perspectives" subsection mentioning Steins;Gate, Your Name, and Korean cinema, but the literature collection still lacks entries for Tsutsui, the Mahabharata's Kakudmi story, or Afrofuturist time-travel works. 6. **Source assessment lacks formal structure.** The literature collection provides one-line annotations but no systematic tiering. A primary/secondary/tertiary classification or a disciplinary-authority assessment would strengthen the scholarly apparatus.
