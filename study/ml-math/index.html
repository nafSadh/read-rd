TEXTBOOK STUDY · APRIL 2026

Mathematics for Machine Learning

Deisenroth, Faisal & Ong — chapter-by-chapter study with interactive deep-dives.

A free, open-access textbook covering the mathematical foundations of ML and their application to four core algorithms. Each chapter is studied through an interactive deep-dive: key definitions, worked examples, geometric intuition, and connections to the ML application chapters.
